**For:** nitingadura.com + gadurarealestate.com
**Status of pilot:** 168 pages live on nitingadura.com (Day 0 = 2026-06-05)
**Purpose:** Off-site moves that boost indexing + ranking without touching the live pilot template

These items are **independent** of the pilot. Even if the Day-14 audit goes poorly and we
roll back, every item below still works — they're permanent moves that strengthen the
domain's authority graph regardless of what's on /market-reports/.

Knock them out at your own pace. None of them require Claude — just you with a credit
card (in some cases) and 15-30 minutes of focus.

## Priority Tier D — Content distribution (passive backlinks, ongoing)

### 16. Substack newsletter "Queens & Long Island Housing Weekly"

- **Why it matters**: Substack pieces index in Google in <24 hours. AI engines cite Substack heavily.
- **URL**: https://substack.com/signup → choose Publication
- **Format**: 1 short newsletter per week summarizing one of your market reports
- **CTA**: Link back to the full nitingadura.com market report
- **Cost**: Free until 1000 paid subscribers

### 17. LinkedIn Articles

- **What**: Cross-post 1-2 paragraphs from each market report with a "Read full report at nitingadura.com" CTA
- **Cadence**: Weekly
- **Why**: LinkedIn pages are indexed by Google + Bing + AI engines fast

### 18. Reddit seeding (long-term, careful)

- **Subreddits**: r/RealEstate (4M), r/AskNYC (500K), r/Queens (40K), r/LongIsland (100K)
- **Strategy**: Become a known helpful commenter for 4-6 weeks BEFORE linking to your site. Just answer real questions. Only later, when you're an established commenter, drop the occasional link.
- **Why**: ChatGPT scrapes Reddit heavily. Mentions of your name + URL become AI training data.
- **DON'T**: Spam-drop URLs. Will get banned + flagged.

### 19. Quora answers

- **URL**: https://www.quora.com/
- **Strategy**: Answer 1-2 NYC/Queens/LI real estate questions per week
- **Why**: Quora answers rank high in Google + are cited by ChatGPT
- **Format**: Real answer first, mention "I'm a NYS licensed agent" once, link at end

### 20. YouTube channel — neighborhood walkthroughs

- **Effort**: HIGH (1-3 hours per video)
- **Cadence**: 1 per month minimum
- **Why**: #2 search engine, gets cited as VideoObject in your market reports' schema
- **Content idea**: "Ozone Park Real Estate in 5 Minutes — June 2026 Market Update"
- **Cross-link**: Embed in your nitingadura.com sell/ pages
