Why NY-specific matters

Generic mortgage calculators don't include the line items that move the math significantly in New York:

  • Mansion tax — kicks in at $1M sale price (1%); climbs to 3.9% at $25M+. Buyer-paid in NY.
  • RPTT (Real Property Transfer Tax) — NYC adds 1% under $500K and 1.425% above; seller-paid.
  • Mortgage recording tax — 1.8% (under $500K) to 1.925% on the loan amount; buyer-paid.
  • NYS transfer tax — $2 per $500 of price (0.4%); seller-paid.
  • SONYMA $15K Down-Payment Assistance — first-time-buyer grant, NY-specific.
  • FHA MIP for life of loan when down payment under 10% — many calcs assume MIP drops at 78% LTV (it doesn't on FHA).
