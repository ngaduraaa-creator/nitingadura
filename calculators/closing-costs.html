NYC Closing Cost Components

NYC closing costs are higher than most US markets — typically 4–6% buyer-side and 8–10% seller-side. Here's what goes into each.

Buyer Closing Costs (NYC)

ItemTypical Range
Mortgage Recording Tax (NY)1.8% under $500K loan / 1.925% on $500K+
Mansion Tax1–3.9% on $1M+ purchases (tiered)
Title Insurance (lender + owner)~0.5–0.8% of purchase price
Buyer's Attorney$1,500–$3,500
Lender Fees (origination, points, application)0.5–1.5% of loan
Inspection$500–$1,000
Appraisal$400–$800

Long Island vs NYC

Long Island purchases skip NYC RPTT but still owe NY State transfer tax + mortgage recording tax. Total LI closing costs typically run 1–2% lower than NYC equivalent.
