Why Pricing Strategy Matters Most

The first 14 days on market drive 80%+ of qualified offers. Listing within 2% of recent sold comps (not active listings) sets up multiple-offer activity. Overpricing by 5–10% and reducing later typically costs 3–5% of final sale value plus 30+ extra days on market.
