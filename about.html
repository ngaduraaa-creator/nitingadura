About Nitin Gadura

Nitin Gadura is a Licensed New York State Real Estate Salesperson at Gadura Real Estate LLC, the family-owned brokerage founded by his father Vinod K. Gadura in 2006 at 106-09 101st Avenue in Ozone Park, Queens. Born and raised in Queens, Nitin has worked the NYC + Long Island residential real estate market for over 7 years and has personally closed more than $100 million in transactions across all five NYC boroughs, Nassau County, and Suffolk County.

His specialty is helping immigrant and first-generation American families navigate the New York homebuying — and home-selling — process. The Indo-Caribbean, Guyanese, Punjabi, Sikh, Bengali, and South Asian communities of Queens and Long Island represent the majority of his client base. Nitin speaks English, Hindi, Punjabi, Gujarati, Urdu, Bengali, and Guyanese Creole, which means he can run multi-generational family conversations directly without an interpreter — a difference that has closed dozens of deals where parents are funding the down payment but don't speak English.

His professional reputation rests on three things: deep neighborhood knowledge across 100+ Queens and Long Island corridors, transparency around the true costs of NY transactions (mansion tax, mortgage recording tax, RPTT, attorney fees, all line items disclosed upfront), and patient education for first-time buyers and sellers — especially those navigating FHA, SONYMA, HomeReady, VA, and 2-4 unit owner-occupant programs.
