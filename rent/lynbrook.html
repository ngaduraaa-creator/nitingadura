NY Tenant Screening — Legal Requirements

NY State requires landlords to:

  • Apply consistent screening criteria to all applicants (no source-of-income discrimination)
  • Accept Section 8, CityFHEPS, SSDI, child-support income equally
  • Provide written denial reason within 30 days if rejecting
  • Cap application fees at $20 per applicant
  • Comply with the NY Tenant Protection Act (rent stabilization rules)
